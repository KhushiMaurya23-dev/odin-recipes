Bhindi Fry Bhaaji-Simple Indian Cooked Ladyfinger

Ingredients:

  • 250 gram of LadyFinger
  • 1 Pan
  • 3 table spoon of Oil
  • 1/2 table spoon of Cumin seed
  • 2-4 Chillies
  • 1/2 or taste according Salt

Instructions:

  1. Clean the LadyFinger in Water, keep it in cloth to let it dry and chooped it in 1 inch small pieces except cut it both edges and throw it in waste
  2. Put your Pan in medium flame, add the Oil, let it hot and add Cumin Seeds
  3. After it become golden brown, add the chopped Ladyfingers in temperent fries and mix it well, cover it, let it cook on medium flame like that for 5 minutes
  4. Grind the Chillies and add it in Pan after 5 min with 50 ml water
  5. Add the Salt accordingly and let it cook in medium flame by covering
  6. Continue checking in between to not let it overcook/burn and let it cook in medium flame for 15 minutes
  7. After it finish cook and your smell aromatic smell, Switch off the flame and your Bhindi Fry Bhaaji is ready! with Roti, Dal and Bhaat
